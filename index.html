ONE OK ROCK

Just give me a reason to keep my heart beating!

One Ok Rock , also written as ONE OK ROCK (pronounced "one o'clock" in Japanese) is a Japanese rock group formed in Tokyo in 2005, currently consisting of six members Takahiro Moriuchi, (Main Vocal), Toru Yamashita (Guitarist, Bandleader), Ryota Kohama (Guitar Bass), Tomoya Kanki (Drummer), Reimon Onizawa (Main Guitarist) and Koyanagi Yu (Drummer) . They are playing a variety of music, from alternative rock , emo , post-hardcore to pop-rock . The group's name, ONE OK ROCK, comes from the phrase "one o'clock", the time they practice on weekends. They choose to work out at one o'clock in the morning because it's cheaper to rent a gym at that time. However, because Japanese doesn't distinguish between "l" and "r" sounds, they changed "O'CLOCK" to "O'CROCK" (or "O'KROCK"), then split it to "OK ROCK" . In addition, the name of ONE OK ROCK is expressed as "10969" (wan-o-ku-ro-ku, the name of ONE OK ROCK is based on the Japanese counting).
